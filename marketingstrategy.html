Marketing and Implementation Strategy

The Company’s management will utilize a comprehensive promotional campaign to drive awareness within the local medical and social services community.

Marketing to referring doctors, nurses, etc., as well as internet marketing, and other methods, will be used. These are further explained below:

Referrals

The strongest marketing channel is hospital referrals in the five-county area, ensuring a steady stream of patients for The Build-Together project. Clients pre-screened by doctors provide all intake information before initial interviews. With clients located at local hospitals, timing difficulties are avoided, leading to increased efficiency.

MARKETING COLLATERAL

The Company has developed attractive product brochures, flyers, and business cards. The Build-Together project also uses direct mail, sending its marketing collateral to a list of roughly 1,000 case managers, nurses, and doctors in the Portland and five-county area.

Our Mission

The mission of Build-Together Project is to design and implement sustainable solutions that address the long-term needs of children with disabilities, fostering their independence, self-worth, and socio-economic integration. We strive to create an inclusive ecosystem that provides comprehensive support, vocational training, educating, enabling children with disabilities to leave fulfilling lives, pursue their dreams, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make a positive impact on the lives of individuals, focusing on children with physically disabilities. We are committed to educate, care, support and pray to build a world where everyone can live with dignity, purpose, and opportunity regardless of their abilities. Together let’s build a world where life is good for everyone and in which all component can find a place.

INTERNET ADVERTISING

The Company dedicates part of its marketing budget to strategic internet marketing, including search engine optimization (SEO). SEO aims to enhance website traffic quality and volume through search engines organically. It also targets various types of searches like image, local, and industry-specific vertical search engines.
